Matrix-Vector Product

This is the example walked through in the Quick Start, which is a 2000 by 2000 Matrix multiplied by a Vector of length 2000. The operation is repeated 100 times to minimize statistical fluctuation. With 4 workers, MathWorkersJS tends to display about a 2-3x speedup relative to the single worker in this example. Tests of this example on mobile device browsers seem to especially benefit!
